About Text Counter Pro

Your comprehensive, free text analysis toolkit designed for writers, students, content creators, and professionals who need precise text metrics and insights.

100% Free Privacy-Focused No Registration Required Instant Analysis

Our Mission

At Text Counter Pro, we believe that powerful text analysis tools should be accessible to everyone. Whether you're crafting the perfect social media post, analyzing document readability, or optimizing content for your audience, our suite of tools provides the insights you need to communicate more effectively.

We're committed to providing fast, accurate, and privacy-respecting text analysis without the need for accounts, subscriptions, or data collection.

Complete Text Analysis Suite

Four powerful tools designed to give you comprehensive insights into your text, from basic counts to advanced readability metrics.

Word Counter

Get detailed word analysis including word count, sentence count, average word length, and identify the most common words in your text.

Features: Unique word count, longest/shortest words, paragraph analysis, words per sentence metrics

Character Counter

Perfect for social media management with character limits for Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn, plus detailed character breakdowns.

Features: Social media limits, punctuation count, character type analysis, non-whitespace counting

Reading Time Calculator

Estimate how long it takes to read your content with customizable reading speed settings, perfect for bloggers and content creators.

Features: Adjustable words-per-minute rate, accurate time estimates, word count integration

Readability Calculator

Analyze your text's readability with Flesch Reading Ease scores, grade level assessments, and passive voice detection for better communication.

Features: Flesch-Kincaid grade level, passive voice percentage, readability scoring
