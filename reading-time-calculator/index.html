Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average reading speed?

The average adult reads at 200-300 words per minute. Our calculator defaults to 250 WPM, which represents typical silent reading speed for most people.

How accurate is this calculator?

Reading time estimates are based on word count and your selected WPM. Actual reading time may vary based on text complexity, familiarity with the topic, and individual reading habits.

Can I adjust the reading speed?

Yes! Update the words per minute (WPM) to match your reading speed. Beginners typically read at 150-200 WPM, while speed readers can exceed 400 WPM.

What types of content work best?

This calculator works for any text content including articles, blog posts, books, documents, and essays. Complex technical content may require slower reading speeds.
