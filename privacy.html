Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Last Updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Text Counter Pro ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and text analysis services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. Your text content never leaves your device and is processed entirely in your browser.

Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information through Google Analytics and advertising services, including:

  • IP address and general location information
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
  • Referral sources and search terms
  • Device information (screen size, device type)

How We Use Information

We use the automatically collected information to:

  • Analyze website usage and improve our services
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Monitor website performance and troubleshoot issues
  • Understand user preferences and behavior patterns

Your Text Content

Privacy First: All text analysis is performed locally in your browser. Your text content is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

Text Counter Pro operates entirely client-side. When you paste or type text into our application, it remains on your device and is processed using JavaScript in your browser. We have no access to your content.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising Services

We may display advertisements through third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdSense, Ezoic, or similar services). These services may:

  • Use cookies and similar technologies to show relevant ads
  • Collect information about your browsing activities
  • Provide personalized advertisements based on your interests

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings page.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website and third-party services may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Deliver targeted advertisements
  • Improve website functionality and performance

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Please note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store personal data or text content, there are minimal security risks. The limited analytics data we collect through third-party services is protected by those services' security measures. We implement reasonable security practices for our website infrastructure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

International Users

Our website is hosted in the United States. If you access our services from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
