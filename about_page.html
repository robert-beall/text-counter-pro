About Text Counter Pro

A free, fast, and accurate word counter for writers and content creators

What is Text Counter Pro?

Text Counter Pro is a free online word counter and text analysis tool designed for writers, students, content creators, and professionals. Get instant word counts, character counts, and text statistics as you type or paste your content.

All text analysis happens locally in your browser, ensuring your content remains private and secure while delivering fast, real-time results.

Key Features

  • Real-time Counting: Instant word and character counts as you type
  • Reading Time: Estimates how long your text takes to read
  • Text Statistics: Sentence count, paragraph count, and average words per sentence
  • Privacy-First: All analysis happens in your browser - your text never leaves your device
  • Mobile Friendly: Works on all devices and screen sizes
  • Completely Free: No registration required, no limits, no hidden costs

Perfect For

Students: Check essay lengths and meet assignment word count requirements.

Content Creators: Optimize blog posts and social media content for platform requirements.

Writers: Track progress and meet client specifications for articles and copy.

Professionals: Craft concise emails and documents that meet length guidelines.

Why Choose Text Counter Pro?

Text Counter Pro provides accurate text analysis with advanced algorithms that handle contractions, hyphenated words, numbers, and special characters. Your privacy is protected since all processing happens locally in your browser, and you can start using it immediately without any registration.
