About WordWise

The free, fast, and accurate word counter trusted by millions of writers worldwide

What is WordWise?

WordWise is a completely free online word counter and text analysis tool designed to help writers, students, content creators, and professionals analyze their text instantly. Whether you're writing an essay, blog post, social media caption, or professional document, WordWise provides accurate word counts, character counts, and detailed text statistics in real-time.

Our tool works entirely in your browser, which means your text never leaves your device. This ensures complete privacy and lightning-fast analysis without any server delays.

Key Features

  • Real-time Word Counting: Get instant word counts as you type or paste text
  • Character Analysis: Count characters with and without spaces
  • Reading Time Calculator: Estimate how long it takes to read your text
  • Sentence & Paragraph Counter: Track document structure and organization
  • Text Statistics: Analyze average words per sentence and text complexity
  • Additional Tools: Case conversion, text formatting, and find & replace
  • Mobile Optimized: Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktop computers
  • Completely Free: No registration, no limits, no hidden fees

About Our Accuracy

WordWise uses sophisticated text parsing algorithms to provide the most accurate word and character counts possible. We handle complex cases including:

  • Contractions (don't, won't, can't)
  • Hyphenated words (state-of-the-art, twenty-one)
  • Numbers and dates (1,000, January 1st, 2024)
  • Abbreviations (Dr., Mr., etc.)
  • Special characters and symbols
  • Multiple languages and character sets

While we strive for perfect accuracy, different style guides may have varying rules for what constitutes a "word." WordWise follows standard conventions used by most word processors and writing platforms.
