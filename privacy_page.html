Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Privacy Overview

Your privacy matters to us. Text Counter Pro is designed to protect your privacy by processing all text analysis locally in your browser.

In short: Your text never leaves your device, we don't collect personal information, and we use standard analytics to improve our service.

Your Text Stays Private

All text analysis happens locally in your browser, which means:

  • Your text is never sent to our servers
  • We cannot see what you're writing or analyzing
  • Your content remains completely private
  • Text is automatically cleared when you leave the page

What Information We Collect

We Don't Collect:

  • Your text content or documents
  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Account data (no registration required)

We May Collect:

  • Anonymous Analytics: Page views, general location, device type
  • Technical Data: Browser type, screen resolution for optimization
  • Usage Statistics: Feature usage to improve the tool

This data is anonymous and cannot identify you personally.

Cookies and Advertising

We may use cookies for:

  • Anonymous analytics (Google Analytics or similar)
  • Displaying relevant advertisements to keep our service free
  • Remembering your preferences locally

Our advertising partners may display personalized ads based on your browsing. You can control cookies through your browser settings or opt out of personalized advertising.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, and content delivery. These services have their own privacy policies and follow industry standards for data protection.

Children's Privacy

Text Counter Pro is safe for all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Students and teachers can use our tool with confidence.

Data Security

Since your text stays on your device, the risk of content-related data breaches is eliminated. For any anonymous data we collect, we use standard security measures and encryption.

International Compliance

Text Counter Pro is designed to comply with privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA. Since we don't collect personal information and your text remains on your device, privacy risks are minimal.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and posted on this page. Continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of any updates.

Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us and we'll respond promptly to privacy-related inquiries.
