Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Privacy Overview

WordWise respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our free word counting and text analysis tool.

The short version: Your text never leaves your device, we don't collect personal information, and we use standard web analytics and advertising practices to keep our service free.

Your Text is Private

WordWise processes all text analysis locally in your web browser. This means:

  • Your text never gets sent to our servers
  • We cannot see what you're writing or analyzing
  • Your documents remain completely private
  • All word counting and text analysis happens on your device

When you close your browser or navigate away from WordWise, any text you entered is automatically deleted from your device's memory.

Information We Collect

Information We DON'T Collect

  • Your text content or documents
  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Account information (no registration required)
  • Files or documents you analyze

Information We MAY Collect

To improve our service and keep it free, we may collect anonymous usage information:

  • Analytics Data: Page views, time spent on site, general location (country/region)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, screen resolution
  • Usage Patterns: Which features are used most, general usage statistics

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Cookies and Local Storage

WordWise may use cookies and local storage for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics or similar services to understand how our site is used
  • Advertising: Ad networks may set cookies to display relevant advertisements
  • Preferences: Remembering your settings (like theme preferences) locally on your device

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality but won't prevent you from using our core word counting features.

Advertising

WordWise displays advertisements to keep our service free. Our advertising partners may:

  • Use cookies to display relevant ads based on your browsing history
  • Collect anonymous information about your visits to our site
  • Show you ads on other websites based on your visit to WordWise

We work with reputable advertising partners who follow industry standards for privacy and data protection. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or ad network preferences.

Current Advertising Partners

We may work with various advertising networks including but not limited to Google AdSense, Media.net, and other certified ad providers. Each partner has their own privacy policy governing their data practices.

Data Security

Since your text never leaves your device, the risk of data breaches involving your content is eliminated. For the limited anonymous data we collect:

  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We regularly review our security practices
  • We never store sensitive personal information

Third-Party Services

WordWise may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymous usage statistics)
  • Advertising: Various ad networks for displaying ads
  • CDN Services: Content delivery networks for faster loading

These services have their own privacy policies. We choose partners who maintain high privacy standards and limit data collection to necessary functions.

Children's Privacy

WordWise is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, children can use our service safely.

Parents and teachers can use WordWise with confidence knowing that student text remains private and secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • We'll notify users of significant changes through our website
  • The updated policy will be effective immediately upon posting

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Contact Us About Privacy

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please contact us. We're committed to transparency and will respond to privacy inquiries promptly.

For privacy-related requests, you can also email us directly at privacy@wordwise.com (replace with your actual contact method).

GDPR and International Users

WordWise is designed to comply with privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other international privacy laws. Since we don't collect personal information and your text never leaves your device, there's minimal privacy risk.

If you're in the EU or other regions with strict privacy laws, you can use WordWise with confidence knowing your data remains on your device.
