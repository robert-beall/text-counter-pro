Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is Text Counter Pro's word count?

Text Counter Pro uses sophisticated text parsing algorithms to provide highly accurate word counts. We handle complex cases like contractions, hyphenated words, numbers, and abbreviations following standard conventions used by most word processors. While 100% accuracy across all style guides isn't possible due to varying rules, Text Counter Pro provides industry-standard accuracy suitable for most writing needs.

Is my text stored or shared anywhere?

No. Text Counter Pro works entirely in your browser, which means your text never leaves your device. We don't store, collect, or share any of the text you analyze. This ensures complete privacy and security for your writing.

Why is Text Counter Pro free?

We believe everyone should have access to quality writing tools. Text Counter Pro is supported through non-intrusive advertising and is designed to remain free forever. There are no hidden fees, premium features, or subscription plans.

Can I use Text Counter Pro offline?

Once Text Counter Pro loads in your browser, the core functionality works offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to initially load the page and access any online features or updates.

Does Text Counter Pro work on mobile devices?

Yes! Text Counter Pro is fully optimized for mobile phones and tablets. The interface adapts to different screen sizes while maintaining all functionality.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

Please use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Include as much detail as possible about the issue, including your browser type, device, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love hearing feature ideas from our users. Use the contact form above and select "Feature Request" as the subject. While we can't implement every suggestion, we carefully consider all feedback.
