Quick Help

Before reaching out, check if your question is answered below:

How accurate is WordWise's word count?

WordWise uses advanced text parsing algorithms and follows standard word counting conventions used by most word processors. Our counts are accurate for contractions, hyphenated words, numbers, and special characters.

Is my text private and secure?

Yes! Your text never leaves your device. All analysis happens locally in your browser, ensuring complete privacy. We cannot see or access your text content.

Why don't I need to create an account?

WordWise works entirely in your browser without requiring server processing. This means no account is needed, and you can start using our tool immediately.

Can I use WordWise offline?

Once the page loads, WordWise works without an internet connection since all processing happens in your browser. However, you'll need an internet connection to initially load the page.

Does WordWise work on mobile devices?

Yes! WordWise is fully responsive and works great on phones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to your screen size for the best experience.
